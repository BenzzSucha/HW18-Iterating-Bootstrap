San Francisco

San Francisco, at the mouth of San Francisco Bay on the Pacific Ocean, is a major hub of international finance, transportation, and culture, evoking images of the iconic Golden Gate Bridge, steep hills, historic cable car system, Chinatown, Victorian houses, and sourdough bread.

Baker Beach

The mile-long Baker Beach lies at the foot of rugged serpentine cliffs west of the Golden Gate. Large waves, undertow. but it provides panoramic views of the Golden Gate Bridge. You can fish or check out the shorelife along the beach and rocky shoreline. Restrooms and picnic tables are nearby.
